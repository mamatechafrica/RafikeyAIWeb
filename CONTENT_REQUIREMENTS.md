## FAQ CONTENT SUGGESTIONS

### Essential FAQ Questions

1. **What is RAFIKEY?**
   RAFIKEY is a confidential AI-powered platform that provides sexual and reproductive health information and support to young people. We offer evidence-based resources, connect users to local services, and provide a safe space for questions and conversations.

2. **Is my information private?**
   Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All conversations are confidential, your data is encrypted, and we never share personal information without your consent. You can use RAFIKEY anonymously or with a username of your choice.

3. **How accurate is the health information?**
   All health information is reviewed by qualified healthcare professionals and based on current medical evidence. However, RAFIKEY is not a substitute for professional medical advice. For urgent health concerns, please consult a healthcare provider.

4. **Can RAFIKEY help me find healthcare services?**
   Yes, RAFIKEY can help you locate youth-friendly healthcare facilities, family planning clinics, and counseling services in your area. We provide information about services, locations, and how to access care.

5. **What if I'm in crisis or need immediate help?**
   If you're experiencing a mental health crisis or emergency, please contact local emergency services or a crisis helpline immediately. RAFIKEY can provide support resources but is not a crisis intervention service.

6. **Is RAFIKEY free to use?**
   Yes, RAFIKEY is completely free to use. Our goal is to make SRHR information and support accessible to all young people regardless of their financial situation.

7. **What topics can I ask about?**
   You can ask about any aspect of sexual and reproductive health including contraception, STI prevention, relationships, consent, menstrual health, pregnancy, and emotional wellbeing. No question is too small or embarrassing.

8. **How do I create an account?**
   You can use RAFIKEY without creating an account for complete anonymity. If you choose to create an account, you'll be able to save conversations and receive personalized recommendations.

9. **Can I talk to a real person?**
   While RAFIKEY primarily uses AI technology, we can connect you with human counselors and peer supporters when needed. We also provide information about local services where you can speak with healthcare professionals.

10. **What if I'm under 18?**
    RAFIKEY is designed specifically with young people in mind, including those under 18. All content is age-appropriate and our privacy protections are especially important for younger users. We respect your autonomy while providing safe, reliable information.

## IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### Content Management
- All user-facing content should be reviewed by SRHR experts
- Testimonials should be carefully anonymized to protect user privacy
- Legal documents should be reviewed by legal counsel familiar with Kenyan data protection laws
- All content should be tested with target demographic for clarity and appropriateness

### Localization Considerations
- Content should reflect Kenyan context and healthcare system
- Consider multiple language support for broader accessibility
- Ensure cultural sensitivity in all messaging
- Include references to local laws and rights

### Regular Updates
- FAQ content should be updated based on user questions
- Blog content should be refreshed regularly
- Contact information should be verified quarterly
- Legal documents should be reviewed annually or when regulations change
